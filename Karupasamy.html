About Me

I am Karupasamy, a passionate farmer who cultivates rice and millets. With a 10th-grade education, I combine traditional farming techniques with innovative practices to ensure sustainability.

Farming is not just a profession for me; it's a way of life. I believe in nurturing the land and preserving our natural resources for future generations. My goal is to produce high-quality crops while maintaining ecological balance.

Farming Practices

My approach to farming includes:

  • Organic Farming: I focus on using organic fertilizers and natural pest control methods to maintain soil health.
  • Crop Rotation: Implementing crop rotation helps in maintaining soil fertility and reducing pests.
  • Water Conservation: I use drip irrigation to conserve water and ensure that my crops receive adequate moisture.
  • Community Engagement: I actively engage with local farming communities to share knowledge and best practices.
